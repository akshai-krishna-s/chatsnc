COLLEGE MANAGEMENT

The college is managed by Sree Narayana Trusts, a corporate management boasting of a long line of tradition and significance, which had contributed to the rich educational and cultural heritage of Kerala. Headquartered at Kollam, Sree Narayana Trusts, was formed by the Founding Manager of Sree Narayana Colleges, Sri. R Sanker; keeping in mind the divine and educational ideals of Sree Narayana Guru and as well as, to ensure the existence of a managerial body, so as to propel and equip the efficiency of the higher educational institutions under it. The Honorable Secretary of the Sree Narayana Trusts, Sri. Vellapally Natesan is the Manager of the College. The Management committee of the college consists of the Members of the executive committee of the Sree Narayana Trusts together with the Principal of the college, who is an Ex-officio member.
