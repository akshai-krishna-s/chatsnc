E-Grantz Scheme

E-Grantz is web-based solution for the timely disbursement of educational grants/concessions to all eligible  SC/ST/OBC/other eligible students including fishermen, instituted by the Government of Kerala. UG/PG/PhD students who are eligible for receiving E -grants Scholarship/fisherman Scholarships will be eligible for all state/central/university scholarships at the same time. The financial assistance reaches the student’s bank account through Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT).

For more details, Contact Section Officer, Scholarship cell or see the latest E-Handbook

The different categories of educational grants/ concessions under Department of Scheduled Castes & Department of fisheries, Govt of Kerala are given below:

Educational concession to the students belonging to the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and Other Eligible Communities including Kudumbies and Converts

Award: For Degree students lump-sum grant Rs. 950. Stipend : Rs. 600/- per month for students who come from places beyond the distance of 8 km. and Rs.530 per month for students who live within 8 km. PG students – lump-sum grant Rs. 675, stipend as above. All students eligible for concession should apply in the prescribed forms with necessary documents within one month of their admission to the college.

How to Apply

The beneficiaries of any educational assistance scheme have to first register online through E-Grantz 3.0  by giving their basic details such as Name, Address, Date of Birth, Gender, Mobile Number, Bank Account details, Aadhar Number, Allotment memo, Caste certificate, Income certificate. Such registered students can apply through the same application for various schemes. Care should be taken while entering the details on-line. Through a single registration, the system could uniquely identify a student and this basic detail shall be used for the disbursement of educational assistance throughout the period of education under various schemes. Beneficiaries can contact nearby AKSHAYA centres for on-line registration.

After the registration process, candidates should submit their application printout with supporting documents to the Scholarship Cell, Sree Narayana College Kollam

Website : https://www.egrantz.kerala.gov.in/
