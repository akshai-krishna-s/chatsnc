Journalism and Mass Communication Department

The Department of Journalism and Mass Communication started functioning in 2014 offers a specialized UG programme BA in Journalism and Mass Communication. The programme includes Journalism and Media Studies, Visual Communication, Public Relations, Advertising, Online Journalism and allied areas along with Language Studies through complementary courses. The Programme gives practical as well as theoretical knowledge in Print, Online, Visual Media and industry-relevant skills with the aid of a well-equipped Media Lab, besides getting the students placed in the fields of Journalism, Advertising and Film.
The department conducts workshops, seminars, industry visits and skill enhancement programmes to keep the students updated and equipped with the changing trends in media and communication. The department also manages and monitors the college radio station RadioLab 2.0, which provides students with a platform to demonstrate their abilities in radio jockeying, content writing, and radio programme production.
