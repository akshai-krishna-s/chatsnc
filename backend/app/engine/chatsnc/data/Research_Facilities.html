Research facilities in Sree Narayana College

Sree Narayana College, Kollam, is a research centre under University of Kerala which offers research programmes in 9 different subjects, English, Hindi, Malayalam, Political Science, Botany, Chemistry, Physics, Zoology and Commerce leading to the Degree of Doctor of Philosophy.

Research facilities in Languages, Commerce and Arts subjects

A collection of wide range of books and journals for the research scholars as reference material for pursuing their research is available in the college library. The collection covers books of contemporary relevance and significance and the areas of research offered by the departments.

Research facilities in Science departments

Well equipped DST,FIST and STAR supported laboratories and a collection of scientific books and journals are available in general library. The N-LIST project provides access to e-resources to students, researchers and faculty through server(s) installed at the INFLIBNET Centre.
