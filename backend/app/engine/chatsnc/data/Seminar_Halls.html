Seminar Halls

The College has three seminar halls

  • G2 Seminal Hall: It is at Ground floor and air conditioned with a seating capacity of 150. Equipped with smart board, LCD projector, FM Microphone, speaker and amplifier sound system

  • S22 Seminar Hall:   It is at Second floor  with a seating capacity of 120

  • Chemistry Seminar Hall: Situated in the Chemistry block of the college with a seating capacity of 50.
